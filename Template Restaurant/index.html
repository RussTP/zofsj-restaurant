About Restaurant

Welcome to Shadyside Grill, where culinary artistry meets the elegance of fine dining. Our restaurant is a haven for those who appreciate the finer things in life. We believe that dining is not just about foodâ€”it's about an experience. Our carefully curated menu features a harmonious blend of traditional and contemporary dishes, expertly prepared on our state-of-the-art grill. Each dish is crafted with the finest ingredients, sourced from local farms and purveyors to ensure the highest quality and freshness.

Founded in 2010, Shadyside Grill has become a beacon of culinary excellence in the heart of Burlington. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a dining experience that combines the artistry of gourmet cuisine with the warmth of exceptional hospitality. The story of Shadyside Grill is one of passion, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our founder, Bob Barker, brought together a team of talented chefs and hospitality professionals who shared a common goal: to push the boundaries of fine dining and delight every guest with unforgettable flavors and impeccable service.
